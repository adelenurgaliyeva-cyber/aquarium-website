Books Publishing in KZ

The books publishing industry in Kazakhstan doesn't have the best numbers.

How many new books appear annually?

According to the Book Chamber, 266 publishers provided their book products in 2021. The majority are located in Almaty (152), Astana (30) and Karaganda (11).
In total, 5,017 new editions of books and brochures were registered. Of these, there are 2963 books in Kazakh, 1910 in Russian.
Most of them belong to scientific and educational publications and manuals – 3865 books. But 865 literary and artistic publications were published during the year, including 187 books of children's literature.


What Are the Costs?

Alina Brovkina is the author of the book "Zyn. The secret of happiness in Shymkent" turned to the publishing house: there they were helped with the illustration, design, layout, the name was chosen and the print run was 1000 copies. All that was required from the author was approval at each stage of the preparation of the manuscript for publication.  It took a total of three months to prepare the book. That was the end of the publishing house's work. It cost the author about three million one hundred thousand tenge.
